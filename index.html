About Me

Welcome! My name is Tom Lindblad and I am an aspiring front-end developer, and you're lucky you just caught me in the beginning of my exciting new journey!

I've been coding on my own for a couple of years, mostly in C# making games with Unity, but in 2024 I grabbed life by the nutsack and applied for a real course in front-end development at Sundsgårdens folkhögskola. And O'boy did the planets align.

Since then I've been in a frenzy to learn everything and anything I can get my hands on about HTML, CSS, JavaScript and more.

So take a peek at some of my projects or see what others have to say about me, and don't be afraid to give me a holla if you got something on your mind! Peace!

//Tom Lindblad
